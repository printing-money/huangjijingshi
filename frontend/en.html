What is this?

Huangji Jingshi (皇极经世, "The Book of Supreme World Ordering") was written by the Song Dynasty philosopher Shao Yong (1011–1077 CE). It maps the entire history of the universe into a mathematical framework of nested time cycles.

How to read the result:
· Yearly Hexagram — The dominant energy pattern of the year, like an annual "weather forecast" for civilization
· Yang Energy — Higher = expansive, active period. Lower = contractive, reflective period
· Five Phases — The relationship between inner and outer trigrams (generating = harmony, controlling = tension)
· Time Layers — From cosmic epoch (129,600 years) down to the current two-hour period

The time hierarchy:
Epoch (129,600 yrs) → Era (10,800 yrs) → Cycle (360 yrs) → Generation (30 yrs) → Year → Month → Day → Hour
Each layer has its own hexagram describing the "climate" of that period.
